About Us

Tapnas Fashions

TAPNAS fashions started in the spring of 2008. A couple had a vision: To spread happiness bh turning an everyday essential into a colorful design piece with a rigid standard of ultimate quality, craftsmanship and creativity. Today TAPNAS products are sold in more than 6 big cities in Zimbabwe. TAPNAS fashions features an almost endless variety of models and designs for womenswear and menswear, using a broad spectrum of color combinations and original patterns. OUR GOAL IS TO PROVIDE YOU WITH THE BEST AND HAPPIEST PRODUCTS AROUND. TAPNAS fashions ensures that its suppliers have high standards by keeping close contact with all of them through regular visits and we pay close attention to the production of our collections and using the best materials and the sharpest controls at all their factories.s
